The brand

Born from the desire to bring to the market a tailoring house that combines tradition and boldness, the brand is shaped by the iconic structure of early twentieth-century elegance.

Founded by Dominic T. Antoniazzi, it reflects transformation, ambition and creative discipline — translating presence into fabric, scent and curated objects.
